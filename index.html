    -Who is our leader?

    Chief Bola Ahmed Adekunle Tinubu GCFR (born 29 March 1952) is a Nigerian politician who is the 16th and current president of Nigeria. He was the governor of Lagos State from 1999 to 2007; and senator for Lagos West in the Third Republic.

     -Why does it affect the citizens?

    The deteriorating economic environment is leaving millions of Nigerians in poverty. On current trends, with Nigeria's population growth continuing to outpace poverty reduction, the number of Nigerians living below the national poverty line will rise by 13 million between 2019 and 2025
